Sichuan · 川菜

Beyond the chili. Sichuan codifies twenty-three flavor profiles — fish-fragrant, strange-flavor, dry-fragrant, lychee, garlic paste — making it the most architecturally rigorous of Chinese cuisines.不止于辣。川菜把味道整理成二十三种味型——鱼香、怪味、糊辣、荔枝、蒜泥——是所有中国菜系里结构最严谨的那一支。

The popular Western shorthand for Sichuan is "spicy." This is a misreading. Sichuan is the cuisine of ma la (麻辣) — a doubled sensation of numbing (麻, from the prickly Sichuan peppercorn) and burning (辣, from the chili) — but ma la is one note among many. The Sichuan Cooking Association formally codified twenty-three flavor profiles (味型, wèi xíng) decades ago. Only seven of them are spicy. The others include "fish-fragrant" (a sweet-sour-pungent profile that contains no fish), "strange-flavor" (a balanced cold sauce hitting all six senses at once), "lychee" (a sweet-and-sour with a particular ratio), and a half-dozen mild profiles for banquet cooking.西方对川菜的简化标签是"辣"。这是个误读。川菜是麻辣的菜——花椒的"麻"与辣椒的"辣"同时作用——但麻辣只是众多味型里的一种。四川烹饪协会几十年前正式整理出二十三种味型，其中只有七种是辣的。剩下的包括鱼香（不含鱼的甜酸辛混合）、怪味（冷盘里六味同时出现的平衡酱）、荔枝（特定比例的糖醋）、以及为宴席而设的好几种清淡味型。

1 · The peppercorn before the chili一 · 辣椒到来之前的花椒

Sichuan was a numbing cuisine for at least a thousand years before it was a spicy one. The Sichuan peppercorn (花椒) — properly Zanthoxylum, related to citrus, not pepper — is native to the basin. Its compound hydroxy-alpha-sanshool produces a vibratory sensation on the lips and tongue. The chili pepper arrived from the Americas in the late 16th century, traveled up from the south coast, and slotted neatly into a cuisine already organized around heat and aroma. The marriage was so successful that it overwrote Western memory of the older, peppercorn-only Sichuan kitchen.在川菜变辣之前，它先做了至少一千年的"麻"菜。花椒（Zanthoxylum，与柑橘同科，不是黑胡椒）是盆地原生。它含的羟基-α-山椒素，在唇舌上产生振动感。辣椒 16 世纪末从美洲经南海岸北上，刚好嵌进一个本来就讲究热与香的菜里。这场结合太成功，让西方人忘了川菜还有"只麻不辣"的更老阶段。

The best Sichuan peppercorn comes from Hanyuan, in the southwest of the province; the harvest runs late July to mid-August. A peppercorn from October is half the seasoning of one from August. Sichuan chefs talk about peppercorn vintages the way wine buyers talk about Burgundy.最好的花椒来自川西南的汉源，采收季在七月底到八月中。十月的花椒，调味力只剩八月的一半。川菜厨子讲花椒年份，跟红酒商讲勃艮第似的。

2 · Pixian doubanjiang — the soul of Sichuan二 · 郫县豆瓣 — 川菜之魂

If Sichuan has a soul ingredient, it is doubanjiang (豆瓣酱) — fermented broad-bean and chili paste — and the best is made in Pixian, just outside Chengdu, in low-ceilinged courtyards filled with massive clay jars. The traditional process: broad beans, salt, chili peppers, and a koji-like wheat starter are layered in jars, exposed to sun by day and dew by night, stirred every few mornings, and left to ferment for at least one summer. Three-year doubanjiang is good. Five-year is reserved. Seven-year is sold by the gram for cooking competitions.川菜真有"魂"，就是豆瓣酱——发酵的蚕豆与辣椒酱——最好出在郫县，成都郊外，露天大缸的院子里。传统工艺是：蚕豆、盐、辣椒、麦曲分层入缸，"日晒夜露"，每隔几天清晨翻一次，至少发酵一夏。三年豆瓣已算好。五年是预留品。七年的按克卖给烹饪比赛。

A serious Sichuan dish — mapo tofu, fish-fragrant pork, twice-cooked pork — begins with doubanjiang fried in oil until the chili turns deep mahogany and the smell fills two rooms. That moment is the dish; everything after is structure. No doubanjiang, no Sichuan kitchen.一道正经川菜——麻婆豆腐、鱼香肉丝、回锅肉——都从郫县豆瓣下油炒香开始，炒到辣椒颜色转深红、香气穿堂为止。这一刻就是这道菜；之后的步骤只是结构。没有豆瓣，就没有川菜。

3 · The 23 flavor profiles, abridged三 · 二十三味型 · 简表

A Sichuan chef trains in the flavor profiles the way a French chef trains in mother sauces. A small selection:川菜厨子练味型，跟法国厨子练母酱一样。挑几个：

  • Ma la (麻辣) — numbing-spicy. The famous one. Mapo tofu, ma la beef.麻辣——出名的那个。麻婆豆腐、麻辣牛肉。
  • Yu xiang (鱼香, fish-fragrant) — sweet, sour, pungent, salty. No fish involved. Built on pickled red chili (泡椒).鱼香——甜、酸、辛、咸。无鱼。底子是泡椒。
  • Guai wei (怪味, strange-flavor) — a cold-dish dressing combining sweet, sour, salty, spicy, numbing, and aromatic, all at once.怪味——凉拌酱，甜、酸、咸、辣、麻、香一锅出。
  • Hu la (糊辣, scorch-spicy) — dried chili briefly burnt in oil. The flavor of kung pao.糊辣——干辣椒在油里略煳。宫保鸡丁就是这个味型。
  • Li zhi (荔枝, lychee) — a sweet-and-sour with a specific 1:1 sugar-to-vinegar ratio that mimics the taste of lychee fruit.荔枝——糖醋按 1:1 调，呈现近似荔枝果味的酸甜。
  • Suan la (酸辣, sour-spicy) — white pepper and dark vinegar, hot but not chili-hot.酸辣——白胡椒与陈醋当家，辣但不是辣椒辣。
  • Suan ni (蒜泥, garlic paste) — raw garlic, soy, chili oil. The dressing for cold sliced pork (蒜泥白肉).蒜泥——生蒜、酱油、辣油。蒜泥白肉就是这个味。

4 · The hot pot, which is its own subject四 · 火锅 · 自成一道

Chongqing hot pot (重庆火锅) — Sichuan's brother city splits the basin with Chengdu — deserves its own treatise. A heavy red broth thick with beef tallow, chilies, peppercorns, fermented black bean, and a closely guarded house spice blend. Diners cook strips of tripe, slivers of beef brain, lotus root, eel, duck blood, and slabs of luncheon meat in the broth, dipping each piece in sesame oil to cool it before eating. The meal is an evening, not a dish.重庆火锅——四川的另一座大城与成都各掌盆地一半——单独可以写一篇。一锅浓重的牛油红汤里，辣椒、花椒、豆豉与店家秘配香料。客人下毛肚、牛脑花、莲藕、鳝鱼、鸭血、午餐肉，每一片在汤里涮过后，先在麻油碟里降温再吃。这是一晚饭，不是一道菜。

Dishes to know这几道，得知道

Mapo tofu麻婆豆腐

má pó dòu fu

Soft tofu, ground beef, doubanjiang, douchi, peppercorn — invented in 1862 by a pockmarked old lady in Chengdu.嫩豆腐、牛肉末、豆瓣、豆豉、花椒——1862 年成都北郊一位脸上有麻点的老太太发明。

Kung pao chicken宫保鸡丁

gōng bǎo jī dīng

Diced chicken, peanuts, scorch-spicy chili, sweet-sour glaze. Named for Qing official Ding Baozhen.鸡丁、花生、糊辣干辣椒、糖醋荔枝味。以清官丁宝桢的"宫保"衔命名。

Fish-fragrant pork鱼香肉丝

yú xiāng ròu sī

Pork slivers in pickled-red-chili sauce — built around the fish-fragrant profile. No fish.猪肉丝，泡椒底子的鱼香味型。不含鱼。

Twice-cooked pork回锅肉

huí guō ròu

Pork belly boiled, then stir-fried with doubanjiang, leek, and fermented bean. The "household" Sichuan dish.五花肉先煮后炒，下豆瓣、青蒜、豆豉。川菜"家常"菜的代表。

Chongqing hot pot重庆火锅

chóng qìng huǒ guō

A red-broth tabletop boil. Eat with sesame oil. Bring patience and bibs.红汤桌上煮。配麻油。带耐心和围裙。

Dan dan noodles担担面

dān dān miàn

A small bowl of noodles in a dark, spicy, sesame-and-vinegar dressing. Originally hawker food.小碗面，黑色辣麻芝麻醋汁。原本是挑担小贩的卖食。
