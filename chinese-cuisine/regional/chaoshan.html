Chaoshan · 潮汕

A small corner of eastern Guangdong with its own dialect, its own opera, and arguably the most particular eaters in China. The brine pot has been simmering, in some shops, since 1932.广东东边一角，有自己的方言、自己的戏曲，以及——可以说是——全中国最挑嘴的食客。卤水锅，有些铺子从 1932 年起就没熄过火。

Chaoshan is two prefectures — Chaozhou and Shantou — pressed between mountains and the Taiwan Strait. The people speak Teochew, a southern Min dialect mutually unintelligible with Cantonese, and have spent four centuries shipping themselves across Southeast Asia. The food, as a result, is two things at once: deeply local (a cuisine of the sea, the brine pot, and an almost theological fixation on freshness) and quietly cosmopolitan (you can taste shadows of Bangkok, Penang, and Saigon in the cooking of any Shantou kitchen).潮汕是潮州、汕头两市，夹在山与台湾海峡之间。讲潮汕话——闽南语支的一种，与粤语互不能听懂。四百年间，潮汕人不停地把自己往东南亚送。食物因此同时是两件事：极地（一门关于海、关于卤水锅、几乎带宗教感的"鲜"信仰的菜），也是低调的世界主义（任何一家汕头馆子里，都能尝到曼谷、槟城、西贡的影子）。

1 · The brine pot — lou shui一 · 卤水

If you walk into an old Chaoshan kitchen the first thing you see is the brine pot — lou shui (卤水) — a deep, near-black, intensely fragrant liquid kept at a low simmer behind the counter. Soy sauce, rock sugar, star anise, cinnamon, dried tangerine peel, Sichuan peppercorn, galangal, several proprietary herbs the chef will not name, and — critically — the cooking liquor of every goose, pork knuckle, and tofu skin ever cooked in it. The pot is never emptied. Each shift, water is topped up and seasoning replenished; the pot itself is the recipe.走进一家潮汕老铺，第一眼是卤水锅——一锅深近黑、香气厚重的卤水，柜台后小火不停。生抽、冰糖、八角、桂皮、陈皮、花椒、南姜，再加上几味老板不愿说的中药材，以及，最关键的——锅里曾经卤过的每一只鹅、每一块猪脚、每一片豆干的汤。锅永不见底。每班加水补料；这锅，本身就是配方。

The most prized brine pots in Shantou are inherited. Three of the city's best are documented to have started in the 1930s; they survived war, revolution, the lean years, and a 1995 fire that almost ended one of them. (The chef rescued the pot, the story goes, before his own savings.) The taste is a thing of accumulation, not of a single recipe.汕头最金贵的卤水锅是传家的。城里有三家可考的可上溯到 1930 年代——熬过了战争、革命、困难年月，1995 年还有一家差点毁于火灾（传说老板先把那锅救出来，自己钱包都没顾上）。这味，是累积出来的，不是某一份配方里能找的。

The defining cut is goose — specifically the lion-head goose of Chenghai, fattened on a strict regimen, slow-simmered for an hour and a half. The skin should be glossy, mahogany. The fat should melt on the tongue. The meat should be tender but not loose. The cut is precise: thin slices for the breast, thicker for the leg, the wings separated at the joint and served whole. Eaten with garlic-vinegar, plain rice congee, a small bowl of mustard greens. That is the meal.招牌一定是鹅——澄海狮头鹅，按章程喂养，文火卤一个半小时。皮要亮，红如老木。脂在舌上化掉。肉嫩而不散。切法讲究：胸肉薄片，腿肉略厚，翅膀关节处分离整上。配蒜醋、白粥、一小碗芥菜。这就是这一餐。

2 · The beef hot pot — by the gram二 · 牛肉火锅 — 按部位上桌

Outside Chaoshan, a beef hot pot means thin slices of brisket. Inside Chaoshan, the menu is a map of the cow. The animal is killed at dawn, in the city or just outside; the meat is at the restaurant by 9 a.m., on the plate by noon, never refrigerated. It is divided into eight to ten named cuts — five-flower (五花趾), grass-strip (匙仁), tendon-mountain (吊龙伴), neck humped, throat fat, and so on — each with its own ideal cook time, ranging from eight seconds (匙仁) to thirty (吊龙伴). A waiter will deliver each cut on its own small plate and time the swish for you, because the wrong duration ruins the meat.潮汕之外，牛肉火锅意味着薄切肥牛。潮汕之内，菜单是一张牛身解剖图。当日清晨屠宰，城里或郊外；九点前到店，中午之前下锅，全程不冷藏。被切成八到十个有名字的部位——五花趾、匙仁、吊龙伴、雪花、嫩肉、胸口朥……每一部位有它最适合的烫时间，从八秒（匙仁）到三十秒（吊龙伴）不等。服务员会一盘一盘送上来，并替你掐表。烫错时间，肉就毁了。

The dipping sauce is, almost defiantly, simple: a small dish of sand-tea sauce (沙茶酱), itself a Chaoshan invention by way of Southeast Asia, made from dried shrimp, peanut, garlic, chili, and toasted spices. There is no soy. There is no chili crisp. The point is the meat.蘸料反倒是固执地简单：一小碟沙茶酱。沙茶本身就是潮汕从东南亚带回来又改造过的发明——干虾、花生、蒜、辣椒、烤过的香料。没有酱油。没有油泼辣子。重点是肉。

3 · Congee at four in the morning三 · 凌晨四点的粥

Chaoshan eats two meals before most cities have started one. The 4 a.m. fish congee shops near the docks are a fixture of Shantou — a thin, savory rice porridge cooked just to the breaking point, finished with slices of nearly-raw fish (yellow croaker, pomfret), shredded ginger, white pepper, and a single drop of sesame oil. The fish is added at the table; the heat of the congee cooks it on contact. Fishermen, taxi drivers, and the occasional insomniac journalist make up the clientele.潮汕人在大多数城市还没开始吃第一顿饭时，已经吃完两顿了。汕头码头边凌晨四点的鱼生粥铺是城市标志——薄、咸口、刚煮到米将开的粥，盛碗后下生鱼片（黄鱼、鲳鱼）、姜丝、白胡椒、滴一滴麻油。鱼下到桌上才入碗，粥的余温把它烫熟。客人是渔民、出租车司机，偶尔还有一两个失眠的记者。

4 · Kung fu tea — the punctuation四 · 工夫茶 — 一顿饭的句读

No Chaoshan meal ends without kung fu tea (工夫茶) — a small clay pot, three thumb-sized cups, oolong of a high grade, brewed and re-brewed at the table by the host. It is not so much a beverage as a punctuation mark; the tea ceremony is the comma between courses, the period at the end of the meal, the colon before a conversation begins. In Chaoshan villages, kung fu tea has the social weight that coffee has in Paris.潮汕饭，没有一顿是不用工夫茶收尾的——一只小陶壶，三个拇指大的茶杯，乌龙茶上品，主人现场冲泡再泡。它不算"饮料"，更像标点：餐与餐之间的逗号，整顿饭末的句号，开始谈话前的冒号。在潮汕乡镇，工夫茶在社交里的分量，跟巴黎的咖啡差不多。

Dishes to know这几道，得知道

Brine-pot goose卤水鹅

lú shuǐ é

The signature dish. A lion-head goose, slow-cooked in a multigenerational brine.招牌。狮头鹅，在传家的卤水锅里慢卤。

Beef hot pot牛肉火锅

niúròu huǒguō

Eight named cuts, each with its own ideal cook time.八个有名字的部位，每一处自有烫秒数。

Raw-fish congee鱼生粥

yú shēng zhōu

Thin rice porridge, near-raw fish, white pepper, sesame oil. Pre-dawn food.米粥薄如水，生鱼片配白胡椒、麻油。黎明前的食物。

Oyster omelette蚝烙

háo luò

Tiny live oysters in a sweet-potato-starch batter, fried until crisp on the outside, gooey within.小活蚝拌薯粉浆，煎到外脆内黏。

Soy-braised pomfret普宁豆酱鲳鱼

dòu jiàng chāng yú

Whole pomfret simmered in Puning soybean paste — the marker of a competent kitchen.整尾鲳鱼以普宁豆酱炖煮——会做这道，馆子就过关了。

Beef-ball noodle soup牛肉丸粿条

niú wán guǒtiáo

Hand-pounded beef balls, springy enough to bounce, in a clear broth with flat rice noodles.手打牛肉丸，弹到能落地反弹，清汤里下扁粿条。

What to drink with it配什么喝

Phoenix Mountain (凤凰山) single-bush oolong (单丛). Among the most complex teas in China — almond, peach, gardenia, depending on the cultivar. A serious Chaoshan kitchen will offer three or four to taste.凤凰单丛——中国最复杂的乌龙之一，依品种不同，能尝到杏仁、桃、栀子。认真的潮汕馆子会上三四款让你试。
