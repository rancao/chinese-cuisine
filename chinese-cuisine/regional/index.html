Angle A · Regional地域路线

Eight kitchens, one continent.八种厨房，一片大陆

The food map of China is not drawn by provinces. It is drawn by rivers, climates, salt routes, and which grain you woke up to. Here are eight of those maps.中国的食物地图不按省界画。它跟着江河、气候、盐道、与你早上醒来吃什么主食走。这里是其中八张。

A note before the map读这张地图之前

There is no "Chinese food." There are kitchens.不存在"中国菜"。存在的是一个个厨房。

"The Eight Great Cuisines" (八大菜系) is a useful starting frame — Lu, Chuan, Yue, Su, Min, Zhe, Xiang, Hui — but it is a 20th-century shorthand. We loosened it. We added Yunnan, which is its own world. We split out Chaoshan from the broader Cantonese umbrella because it deserves its own page. We made room for the Northwest, the Northeast, and Hainan, which the canonical eight tend to under-serve. Eight is a beautiful number; it should not be a cage."八大菜系"（鲁、川、粤、苏、闽、浙、湘、徽）是个好用的起点，但它毕竟是 20 世纪的简略说法。我们松了它一下：加入云南，它自成一界；把潮汕从粤菜里抽出来单写，它值得自己的一页；为西北、东北、海南腾出位置——这几片在八大里常被压扁。八是好数字，但不应该变成笼子。

Southwest highlands西南高地

A mountain larder of wild mushrooms, ham, rice noodles, sour-spicy Dai food, and a calendar of foraged things you have never heard of.一座山间食材库——野生菌、火腿、米线、傣味的酸辣，以及一份外人没听过的"采集日历"。

The deeper logic底层逻辑

Three axes that draw the map画出这张地图的三根轴

1. Rice or wheat米与麦

The Qinling–Huai line, roughly horizontal across central China, is the staple boundary. South of it, rice. North of it, wheat. The line predicts whether your local kitchen is full of noodles, dumplings, and breads — or full of rice bowls, congee, and rice noodles.秦岭—淮河一线，横贯中部，是主食的分界。线南吃米，线北吃麦。这条线决定了你家厨房里是面条饺子馒头，还是米饭粥米粉。

2. Sea, river, or land海、河、与陆

Chaoshan and Min cook from the South China Sea. Jiangnan cooks from the Yangtze. Lanzhou cooks from a desert frontier. Where the protein swims, walks, or grazes is the second axis — and it determines almost everything downstream.潮汕、闽吃南海。江南吃长江。兰州吃边陲。蛋白质是游的、是走的、还是吃草的，是第二根轴——它几乎决定了下游的一切。

3. Salt or chili盐与辣

Before the chili arrived (in the 16th century, from the Americas via Manila and Macau), heat in China was made from black pepper, ginger, mustard, and the Sichuan peppercorn. The chili reshaped four provinces in two centuries. Where it landed, and where it did not, is the third axis.辣椒到来之前（16 世纪经马尼拉与澳门从美洲传入），中国的辣是黑胡椒、姜、芥与花椒做出来的。两百年间，辣椒改写了四个省。它在哪里落地、又在哪里没落地，是第三根轴。
