Northwest · 西北

Where China stops being rice and becomes bread. Hand-pulled noodles, mutton, naan, cumin, and the long arc of Hui Muslim cooking that connects Xi'an to Kashgar.中国从米转向麦的地方。拉面、羊肉、馕、孜然，以及一条从西安到喀什、跨越两千公里的回族饮食长弧。

"Northwest" is a loose label for a vast, dry quarter of China: Shaanxi, Gansu, Ningxia, Qinghai, and Xinjiang. The shared substrate is wheat — bread, noodles, dumplings — and lamb. The shared cultural undercurrent is the Hui (回, Chinese-speaking Muslim), whose dietary practices reshaped the region's protein. Beyond Xinjiang, the cuisine becomes Uyghur, an entire culinary world of its own: hand-stretched laghman, fragrant pilaf, samsa baked in tandoor-like ovens. A serious Northwest meal is rarely small and almost never quiet."西北"是一个松散的标签，覆盖陕、甘、宁、青、新一片巨大的干燥地带。共同底色是麦——馍、面、饺——和羊。共同的文化暗流是回族（讲汉语的穆斯林），他们的饮食规范重塑了这一带的蛋白选择。再往西到新疆，就进入维吾尔族的另一个食物宇宙：拉条子、手抓饭、馕坑里烤的萨木萨。一顿正经的西北饭，几乎从来不少，也几乎从来不安静。

1 · Lanzhou hand-pulled beef noodles一 · 兰州牛肉面

A bowl of Lanzhou hand-pulled noodles (兰州牛肉面) follows the strict rule of "one clear, two white, three red, four green, five yellow" — clear broth, white radish, red chili oil, green cilantro and scallion, and the slightly golden noodles themselves. The pull is theater: the master stretches and slaps a rope of dough until it doubles itself eight, nine, ten times — each doubling halving the thickness. Locals order by width: capillary (毛细), thin (细), middle (二细), wide (宽), or knife-cut (大宽). Each demands a different broth temperature and a different chewing rhythm.一碗兰州牛肉面要遵守"一清二白三红四绿五黄"的规矩——清汤、白萝卜、红辣子、绿香菜葱花、面身微黄。拉面是表演：师傅把一根面剂反复抻、摔，对折八九十次，每对折一次粗细减半。本地人按粗细点：毛细、细、二细、宽、大宽。每一种对应不同的汤温与咀嚼节奏。

A correct Lanzhou bowl arrives quickly: noodles dropped fresh-pulled into boiling broth for 8–12 seconds, then ladled into a bowl with beef shank cut paper-thin, a swirl of chili oil, a fistful of scallion-cilantro. You eat it at the counter standing or on a low stool, often before noon. It is a breakfast dish.合格的一碗上得快：现拉现下，8 到 12 秒出锅，配薄如纸的牛腱片、一勺红油、一把葱花香菜。多半是站在柜台前或坐矮凳上吃，往往在上午十一点前。这是早餐。

2 · Xi'an — biangbiang, roujiamo, liangpi二 · 西安 — 油泼面、肉夹馍、凉皮

Xi'an, the Tang capital, ate noodles a thousand years before Marco Polo arrived. Biangbiang noodles — wide as a belt, hand-slapped, finished with chili oil and a spoonful of black vinegar — take their name from an onomatopoeia (the sound the dough makes on the counter) and a Chinese character so complex that calligraphers debate which dictionary even lists it. Roujiamo (肉夹馍), the "Chinese hamburger," is older than the Western hamburger by about two millennia: chopped slow-cooked pork or beef stuffed into a charcoal-fired flatbread (白吉馍). And liangpi (凉皮) — cold rice-flour noodles, dressed with sesame paste, garlic water, chili oil, vinegar — is the city's signature hot-weather food.西安，唐朝京都，比马可·波罗早一千年就在吃面了。biangbiang 面——宽如腰带，手摔，油泼配一勺香醋——名字来自一个拟声词（面摔在案板上的声音），以及一个复杂到字典学家都争论"哪本字典收过它"的字。肉夹馍——"中国汉堡"——比西方汉堡早大约两千年：慢炖的猪肉或牛肉切碎，塞进炭火烤的白吉馍。凉皮——冷的米皮，淋芝麻酱、蒜水、辣油、醋——是这座城热天的招牌。

3 · Xinjiang — lamb, cumin, the tandoor三 · 新疆 — 羊、孜然、馕坑

In Xinjiang the cuisine is Uyghur, Central Asian, and only nominally Chinese. The center of the meal is lamb — sometimes mutton — grilled on a skewer over chili-and-cumin coals, or stewed in a heavy iron pot with potatoes and carrots and pulled noodles (大盘鸡, "big plate chicken," which can also be lamb), or layered with rice and dried apricot in a pilaf called polo (抓饭). The bread is naan (馕), baked stuck to the wall of a tandoor (馕坑). The drink is salt-milk tea, or, increasingly, a watermelon eaten with a knife.新疆的菜是维吾尔的、中亚的、只在名义上算中餐。一顿饭的中心是羊——有时是绵羊——铁签子上撒孜然辣椒在炭火上烤；或在厚铁锅里和土豆胡萝卜以及拉条子一起炖（大盘鸡——也可以用羊）；或与米、杏干层层叠叠做成手抓饭。主食是馕，烤在馕坑壁上。喝的是奶茶加盐，或者越来越流行——一只西瓜，刀切而食。

4 · Ningxia & Qinghai — lamb and the high plateau四 · 宁夏与青海 — 羊与高原

Less known but no less interesting. In Ningxia, Hui kitchens turn out hand-grabbed lamb (手抓羊肉) — a whole leg, only salt and a single onion, boiled until it lifts off the bone, eaten with the fingers. In Qinghai, the cuisine begins to fade into Tibet: yogurt, barley flour (tsampa), butter tea, and yak-meat dumplings. The altitude rewrites flavors. Salt is more pronounced. Fat is necessary, not optional.名声较小但同样有意思。宁夏的回族厨房做手抓羊肉——一整条腿，只下盐与一只洋葱，煮到肉离骨，徒手撕食。青海再往西，菜系开始向藏区过渡：酸奶、糌粑、酥油茶、牦牛肉饺子。海拔改写了味道。盐的存在感更强。脂肪是必需的，不是可选项。

Lanzhou beef noodles兰州牛肉面

lánzhōu niúròu miàn

Hand-pulled, clear-broth, breakfast of a million Lanzhouers.拉面、清汤，百万兰州人的早餐。

Roujiamo肉夹馍

ròu jiā mó

Slow-braised pork (or beef) packed into a charcoal-fired flatbread.慢炖的猪肉（或牛肉）夹在炭烤白吉馍里。

Biangbiang noodlesbiangbiang 面

biáng biáng miàn

Belt-wide hand-slapped noodles, oil-poured, vinegar-edged.腰带宽的手摔面，油泼，醋点。

Lamb skewers羊肉串

yáng ròu chuàn

The Xinjiang version: cubes of fat alternating with cubes of meat, heavy cumin, smoke.新疆式：肥瘦相间方块，大量孜然，烟熏。

Polo / pilaf手抓饭

zhuā fàn

Rice cooked with lamb fat, carrot, dried apricot, mutton on the bone.米饭与羊油、胡萝卜、杏干、带骨羊肉一起焖。

Liangpi cold noodles凉皮

liáng pí

Wheat-starch sheets, sesame paste, garlic water, chili oil, dark vinegar.小麦淀粉皮，配麻酱、蒜水、辣油、陈醋。
