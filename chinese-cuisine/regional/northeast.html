Northeast · 东北

Liaoning, Jilin, Heilongjiang. Sauerkraut by the barrel. Pork by the slab. Cold so deep the dumplings get stored on the windowsill. Russia, Korea, and the Manchurian winter all sit at the table.辽、吉、黑。酸菜按缸算。猪肉按板算。冷到饺子摆在窗台上当冰箱。俄罗斯、朝鲜、与一整个满洲的冬天，都坐上了桌。

Dongbei — the Northeast — is a cuisine of winter. The growing season runs from May to September; the rest of the year, the ground is iron. Until refrigeration arrived (and even today in villages), the way you ate vegetables in February was to have preserved them in October: heads of cabbage submerged in saltwater under stones, root vegetables buried, mushrooms strung up by the door. The food is built on these preserved foundations.东北菜是冬天的菜。生长季只有五到九月；剩下大半年，地是铁的。冰箱普及以前（村里至今）二月还能吃到菜，是十月就要腌好的：白菜整颗压在盐水里、根菜埋进地窖、菌子挂在门口风干。这门菜的地基，是这些腌制品。

1 · Sauerkraut and pork — suancai bairou一 · 酸菜白肉

The defining dish, in our view, is suancai bairou (酸菜白肉) — sauerkraut-and-pork stew. A whole cabbage is fermented in brine for thirty to forty days; the result is sour, crunchy, and aromatic in a way no quick pickle can reach. Pork belly is sliced thick, blanched, sliced again, layered with the kraut, and simmered in a heavy iron pot with shaoxing wine, a little dried shrimp, glass noodles, and sometimes blood pudding. It cooks slowly, eats hot, and forgives being kept on a low flame all afternoon. The variant suancai dun fenliao (酸菜炖粉条) — sauerkraut and glass noodles, no meat — is the workman's version.代表作我们认为是酸菜白肉——一整颗白菜以盐水发酵三十到四十天，得到的酸是脆的、香的，速腌做不出来。五花肉厚切，焯水，再切，与酸菜层层叠放，进厚铁锅，加黄酒、一点虾米、粉条，有时下一块猪血。慢炖、热吃、整个下午挂在小火上不嫌久。变体酸菜炖粉条——无肉版——是工人的日常。

2 · Big plate, no apology — guo bao rou二 · 大盘菜 — 锅包肉

A Northeastern restaurant has a particular plate-shape: enormous and round. Portions are not pretty; they are abundant. Guo bao rou (锅包肉) — Harbin's defining dish — is a perfect example: thin-sliced pork loin double-fried in starch batter, then flash-tossed in a glaze of vinegar, sugar, ginger, and a touch of tomato. Crisp, sweet-sour, served on a platter so large that two people cannot finish it. The dish was invented in 1907 by a chef cooking for Russian guests in Harbin, who liked things sweeter than the Chinese palate of the time. It stuck.东北馆子的盘子有个特点：大、圆。装盘不讲究漂亮，讲究多。锅包肉——哈尔滨的代表作——是个好例子：里脊薄片裹淀粉糊复炸两遍，再急火炒入一抹醋、糖、姜、一点番茄汁的糖醋汁里。脆、酸甜，一盘大到两个人吃不完。这道菜 1907 年由一位哈尔滨厨子为俄国客人发明，俄国人喜欢甜——比当时中国口味偏甜——做法就此固定下来。

3 · The Russian shadow — Harbin in particular三 · 俄罗斯阴影 — 尤其是哈尔滨

Harbin was a Russian railway town before it was a Chinese metropolis. Its bakery culture descends directly: hong chang (红肠), a Chinese-Russian smoked sausage with garlic and paprika, is sold by the meter on Central Avenue. Da lieba (大列巴), a sourdough rye loaf weighing two kilograms, was for decades baked in the Harbin railway bakery's wood-fired ovens to a recipe that came east from Vladivostok. Even kvass (格瓦斯) — a mildly alcoholic fermented bread drink — is sold in plastic bottles in Heilongjiang supermarkets, more popular there than in Russia itself.哈尔滨在做中国大城市之前，是个俄国的铁路城市。它的烘焙文化是直接继承下来的：红肠（带蒜与辣椒粉的中俄式烟熏肠）在中央大街按米卖。大列巴——一只两公斤的酸面包——曾在哈尔滨铁路面包厂的柴火窑里按从海参崴东来的方子烤了几十年。连格瓦斯——一种微酒精的发酵面包饮料——在黑龙江的超市里塑料瓶装卖，在那儿比在俄罗斯本土还流行。

4 · The Korean-Chinese kitchen — Yanbian四 · 朝鲜族的厨房 — 延边

In Jilin's far east, the Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefecture has been a Chinese-Korean kitchen for over a century. Cold noodles (冷面) — buckwheat noodles in icy beef broth with cucumber, pickled radish, half a hard-boiled egg, a slice of pear — are a summer fixture, served almost as a beverage. Mingtai-fish stew (明太鱼汤), grilled marinated beef (烤牛肉), and a dozen banchan-like cold dishes on the table. The cuisine is genuinely Korean — born of the same ingredients, ferments, and techniques — but cross-pollinated for a century with Northeast Chinese cooking.吉林东部的延边朝鲜族自治州，已经是一个汉朝鲜共生的厨房一百多年了。冷面——荞麦面在冰镇牛肉汤里，配黄瓜、腌萝卜、半只水煮蛋、一片梨——夏天近乎一种饮料。明太鱼汤、腌烤牛肉、十几碟类似 banchan 的小凉菜。这门菜是地道的朝鲜菜——同样的食材、发酵和技法——但与东北菜彼此交换了一个世纪。

Dishes to know这几道，得知道

Sauerkraut & pork酸菜白肉

suān cài bái ròu

Fermented cabbage and pork belly in iron pot. Winter incarnate.酸菜与五花肉在铁锅里。冬天的化身。

Guo bao rou锅包肉

guō bāo ròu

Double-fried pork in a sweet-sour glaze. The Harbin show-stopper.复炸的里脊裹糖醋汁。哈尔滨的招牌响声。

Pork & cabbage dumplings猪肉白菜饺子

jiǎozi

The northern household dumpling. Cabbage's water-holding ability is the technique.北方人家的饺子。白菜的含水率，是这道菜的技术。

Yanbian cold noodles延边冷面

lěng miàn

Buckwheat noodles in icy beef broth. A summer drink in a bowl.荞麦面下冰牛汤。夏天的"碗装饮料"。

Di san xian地三鲜

dì sān xiān

The "three earth-treasures": potato, eggplant, green pepper — flash-fried, soy-glazed."地里的三鲜"：土豆、茄子、青椒，过油后下酱汁。

Tie guo dun (iron-pot stew)铁锅炖

tiě guō dùn

A whole chicken or fish in iron, with cornmeal pancakes pressed onto the inside rim to steam.整鸡或整鱼炖一铁锅，玉米面饼贴在锅边上同蒸。
