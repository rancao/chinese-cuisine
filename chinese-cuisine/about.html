The project本志缘起

About this atlas.关于这本食典

Why we built a slow, bilingual field guide to Chinese cuisine instead of yet another recipe blog.为什么我们不做又一个食谱博客，而做一本慢慢的、双语的中国饮食田野志。

There is a peculiar bookshelf problem with Chinese food in English. Bookstores carry many cookbooks. Search engines surface many recipes. But there is almost no patient writing about why kelp goes into a Cantonese stock, what a Hui-Muslim cleaver in Lanzhou is shaped to do, or how the Hakka people, displaced for centuries, kept their cooking alive on the road.英文世界写中国菜有个奇怪的"书架问题"。食谱书架满满当当，搜索引擎一搜一大把。但少有耐心的写作——写为什么粤式高汤里要放海带，兰州的回族刀具为什么是那个形状，迁徙数百年的客家人怎么在路上把家里的味道留住。

This site is our attempt at that missing shelf.这个站，就是我们试着补上的那一格书架。

Five angles, on purpose五个角度，是有意为之

Most coverage of Chinese food in English collapses into the same dozen dishes — General Tso, kung pao, fried rice, dumplings, mapo tofu. We chose five lenses to fight that flattening.英文写中国菜，往往只反复写那十几道——左宗棠鸡、宫保鸡丁、炒饭、饺子、麻婆豆腐。我们刻意用五个镜头打破这种扁平。

  • Regional.地域。 Not "Chinese food" but Yunnan, Chaoshan, Hakka, the Northwest. The grain belt is not the rice belt is not the mountain belt.不是"中国菜"，而是云南、潮汕、客家、西北。麦区不是稻区，稻区也不是山区。
  • Street.街食。 What city eats at night. Carts and stalls, not banquet halls.城市夜里吃什么。摊车与小铺，不是宴席。
  • Ingredient & craft.食材与技艺。 The pantry. Fermentation. The knife. The fire.食材柜。发酵。刀。火。
  • Cross-cultural.跨文化。 What the diaspora kept, what it invented, what it had to.流散的中餐保留了什么，发明了什么，又被迫改变了什么。
  • Seasonal.节令。 The calendar made edible: Qingming, the Dragon Boat, the Winter Solstice.把日历做成可以吃的——清明、端午、冬至。

House rules编辑底线

A short list of things we try not to do, written down so we can be held to them:一份"我们尽量不做"的清单，写下来，方便日后回头查。

  1. No "authentic" police. Diaspora dishes are dishes. American Chinese food is American food. We will say what is what, but we will not rank them.不当"正宗"的警察。流散菜也是菜。美式中餐就是美式中餐。我们指出谁是谁，但不排座次。
  2. No recipes without a reason. If a recipe appears, it is because the technique inside it explains something larger.不无端写食谱。若出现，必因其中的技法能解释更大的事。
  3. Bilingual or nothing. Every page must read in both languages without one feeling like a translation of the other.要么双语，要么不发。每一页中英都要读得顺，不能让一边看起来像翻译稿。
  4. Slow. We would rather publish nothing this month than publish thin.慢。这个月没东西可发，也不发凑数的。

A note on photographs关于照片

The imagery throughout this site is sourced from open photography archives during the bootstrapping phase. As the project matures, we will replace these with original, location-stamped photographs taken by the editors during reporting trips.起步阶段，本站用开放图库的照片。随着栏目长大，我们会陆续换成编辑实地拍摄、带地名时间戳的原片。

Reach us联系我们

Tips, corrections, your aunt's secret pickled mustard greens recipe — all welcome. This is a quiet little publication; we read everything.线索、勘误、你阿姨腌雪菜的祖传配方，都欢迎。我们是个小小的出版物，每封信都会读。
