Angle B · Street Food街食与夜市

After the office, after the curfew.下了班之后，宵夜之前

Restaurants serve the city's daytime self. Carts and stalls serve the rest of it. A field log of how Chinese cities actually eat after dark.餐厅服务城市的白天，摊车与小铺服务它剩下的时间。这里是中国城市入夜之后真实吃法的一份田野记录。

Why the street, not the kitchen为什么写街道，不写厨房

The cart is the test of a city.一座城的好坏，问它的摊车

A great Chinese restaurant exists for tourists, expense accounts, and the photographable banquet. A great street stall exists for the kid coming home from cram school, the welder finishing a night shift, the cab driver between fares. The street kitchen has to be good or no one eats there twice. We follow the carts.好的中餐馆是给游客、给公账、给打卡照片的。好的街边小摊是给补习回家的孩子、下夜班的焊工、空车时的司机。街边那一锅必须好，否则没人吃第二次。我们跟着摊车走。

House rules for the cart摊车守则

How we judge a street stall我们怎么评一个摊子

  1. Queue of locals, no English menu. If signage is bilingual, lower your expectations one notch.本地人排队，无英文菜单。 招牌带英文翻译的，期待值降一档。
  2. One thing, done forever. A stall that specializes in three dishes is doing well. A stall that specializes in one dish for thirty years is the cart you want.一样东西做到老。 做三样的可以吃。做一样做了三十年的，才是要找的那一家。
  3. Watch the turnover, not the line. A queue can be tourists. Velocity of food through the pass is a better signal.看出餐速度，不只看队伍。 队伍可能是游客。一锅里东西出得快，才说明买的人真懂。
  4. The chair test. Wobbly plastic stool means the rent is in the food, not in the room.椅子测试。 摇摇晃晃的塑料矮凳，意味着房租花在了食物上，不在装修。
  5. The oil. Cheap food and good oil rarely coexist; if it smells right, eat. If it doesn't, walk on.闻油。 便宜食物与好油同时出现的概率极低。油闻着正，就吃；闻着不对，走人。
