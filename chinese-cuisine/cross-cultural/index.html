"Inauthentic" is a lazy word."不正宗"是个偷懒的词

A General Tso's chicken in Queens is not "fake Chinese food." It is a real dish — an American-Chinese dish, invented under American constraints, served to American tongues, by Chinese-American cooks doing the considerable work of survival. The right question is never "is it authentic." The right questions are: where did it come from, what does it tell us about both kitchens, and is it any good?皇后区的左宗棠鸡不是"假中餐"。它是一道真菜——美式中餐——在美式条件下、为美式口味发明、由华裔厨子在艰难谋生中做出来的。值得问的从来不是"是否正宗"，而是：它从哪儿来，它同时告诉我们什么关于两边的厨房，以及——它好吃吗？
