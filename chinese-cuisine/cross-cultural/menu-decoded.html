A vocabulary · A field guide菜单的词汇表

The Chinese menu, decoded中文菜单解读

A Chinese menu is more readable than it looks. Most dish names are constructed out of about fifteen verbs and ten adjectives. Learn those, and you can predict 80% of what arrives.中文菜单比它看上去好读。大部分菜名是用十五个动词加十个形容词搭出来的。学会这二十五个字，端上来什么你大概能猜中 80%。

A Chinese dish name is, more often than not, a small recipe in itself. It will tell you: what the cooking technique was, what the main ingredient is, and (sometimes) what flavor profile to expect. The grammar runs technique + ingredient, or flavor + ingredient. Once you can parse fifteen verbs and ten adjectives, you have decoded most of the menu. This essay is that vocabulary.中文菜名，大半是一份小食谱。它告诉你：用了什么技法、主料是什么，有时还告诉你味型。语法是"技法+食材"或"味型+食材"。学会十五个动词与十个形容词，菜单就读得通大半。这一篇就是这份词汇表。

The fifteen verbs十五个动词

Chinese cooking has codified its techniques. The technique is almost always the first character or two in a dish name. Memorize these and you know what the kitchen did to the food.中餐把技法分得很细。技法通常就是菜名的第一两个字。背下这些，你就知道厨房对食物做了什么。

炒 chǎo

Stir-fry. The default. High heat, oil, constant motion. 90 seconds.翻炒。 默认技法。猛火、油、不停翻。90 秒。

爆 bào

Blast-fry / flash-fry. Stir-fry's louder cousin. Brisk, brief, often using harder ingredients (kidney, squid).爆炒。 炒的更猛版本。短促、急火，常用韧料（腰、鱿）。

烧 shāo

Braise, "cook down." Long simmer with sauce, reducing toward a glaze. Red-braised (红烧) is the famous variant: dark soy, sugar, star anise.烧。 长焖收汁，往釉感上做。红烧是它的明星变体：老抽、糖、八角。

炖 dùn

Stew. Lower-temperature, longer, more broth retained.炖。 温度更低、时间更长、汤水保留更多。

蒸 zhēng

Steam. The Cantonese fish technique. Pure, retains shape and color.蒸。 粤式清蒸的核心。形与色不变。

煮 zhǔ

Boil. The simplest technique. Often paired with a powerful dipping sauce.煮。 最朴素的技法。常配重口蘸料。

煎 jiān

Pan-fry. Low oil, contact-cooked on a flat surface.煎。 少油，贴平面烙熟。

炸 zhá

Deep-fry. Submerged in oil. The frame for sweet-sour pork, General Tso's, sesame chicken.炸。 油里完全淹没。糖醋肉、左宗棠、芝麻鸡的底子。

烤 kǎo

Roast / grill. Dry heat. Peking duck is kǎo. Lamb skewers are kǎo.烤。 干热。北京烤鸭是烤；羊肉串也是烤。

卤 lǔ

Brine-simmer. The Chaoshan technique. A complex master stock simmers the meat over time.卤。 潮汕的招牌。复合老汤慢卤。

烩 huì

Stew-style stir-fry. Multiple ingredients combined in a starch-thickened sauce.烩。 多料同烩，勾芡收汁。

烫 / 涮 tàng / shuàn

Blanch / hot-pot dip. Brief immersion in boiling broth. The technique of the hot pot table.烫 / 涮。 短暂入沸汤。火锅桌上的技法。

拌 bàn

Cold-toss. Pre-cooked or raw ingredients dressed with sauce. Most cold appetizers.拌。 凉拌。多用于头盘冷碟。

泡 pào

Pickle / soak. A long-soak in brine, vinegar, or wine. Drunken crab is pào.泡。 长时间浸泡。醉蟹的"醉"就是 pào 的远亲。

焗 jú

Salt-bake / closed-environment cook. Hakka salt-baked chicken is the canonical jú.焗。 密闭高温焗熟。客家盐焗鸡是教科书。

烟熏 yān xūn

Smoke. Less common in Chinese cooking; used in Hunan and Sichuan.烟熏。 中餐里不算常见；湘、川偶用。

The ten adjectives十个形容词

When a dish name has a flavor adjective rather than (or in addition to) a verb, it is telling you which of the codified flavor profiles the kitchen is hitting. These are mostly Sichuan vocabulary, since Sichuan codifies most aggressively, but they appear all over.菜名上有味型形容词的，是在告诉你这道菜要打哪个味型的"靶心"。这些词大半来自川菜（川菜分得最细），但全国通用。

  • 红烧 hóng shāo — "red-cooked." Dark soy + sugar + star anise. The defining home-style braise.红烧——老抽、糖、八角。家常红烧的标准。
  • 鱼香 yú xiāng — "fish-fragrant." Sweet, sour, spicy, garlicky. No fish.鱼香——甜酸辣蒜。不含鱼。
  • 麻辣 má là — "numbing-spicy." Sichuan peppercorn + chili.麻辣——花椒加辣椒。
  • 家常 jiā cháng — "home-style." Familiar, unfussy, often slightly spicy and salty.家常——家里做法，质朴、常带咸辣。
  • 宫保 gōng bǎo — a name and a flavor: scorch-spicy dried chili, peanuts, sweet-sour glaze.宫保——既是名字也是味型：糊辣干椒、花生、糖醋荔枝。
  • 糖醋 táng cù — "sugar-vinegar." Sweet-and-sour. Pork, fish, ribs.糖醋——糖醋。肉、鱼、排骨。
  • 蒜泥 suàn ní — "garlic paste." Raw garlic dressing for cold sliced pork.蒜泥——生蒜调料。蒜泥白肉。
  • 酸辣 suān là — "sour-spicy." White pepper + black vinegar.酸辣——白胡椒加陈醋。
  • 清蒸 qīng zhēng — "clear-steamed." Cantonese fish.清蒸——粤式蒸鱼。
  • 白切 bái qiē — "white-sliced." Cantonese poached chicken, served cold-sliced with three dips.白切——粤式白切鸡，凉切配三蘸料。

Reading a menu in practice实际看菜单

A few worked examples. 红烧肉: red-cooked + meat (pork by default) → pork belly braised in dark soy, sugar, star anise. 鱼香茄子: fish-fragrant + eggplant → eggplant in pickled-chili sauce, no fish. 麻辣牛肉: numb-spicy + beef → beef in chili and peppercorn. 清蒸石斑鱼: clear-steamed + grouper → whole steamed grouper, Cantonese style. 盐焗鸡: salt-bake + chicken → Hakka whole chicken roasted under salt. 糖醋排骨: sweet-sour + ribs → fried pork ribs in a sticky vinegar-sugar glaze.几个例子。红烧肉：红烧 + 肉（默认猪）→ 五花肉炖老抽糖与八角。鱼香茄子：鱼香 + 茄子 → 泡椒味茄子，无鱼。麻辣牛肉：麻辣 + 牛肉 → 川式花椒辣椒底牛肉。清蒸石斑鱼：清蒸 + 石斑 → 粤式整鱼清蒸。盐焗鸡：盐焗 + 鸡 → 客家整鸡盐焗。糖醋排骨：糖醋 + 排骨 → 炸排骨裹甜醋汁。

Once the pattern lands, you no longer "read" a menu; you parse it. The 200-item menu of a busy Sichuan restaurant collapses into a 15×10 grid you can scan in twenty seconds and order from confidently.一旦看出这个模式，菜单就不再是"读"——是"解析"。一家川菜馆 200 个菜的大菜单，会缩成一个 15×10 的格子；二十秒扫完，从容下单。
