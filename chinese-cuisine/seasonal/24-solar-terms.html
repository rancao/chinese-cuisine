The 24 solar terms二十四节气

The agricultural calendar that has guided Chinese eating for two millennia. Every 15 days, a name, a weather phenomenon, a food. The year, in food.指导中国人怎么吃了两千年的农事日历。每 15 天一个名字，一种天气现象，一道食物。一年，用食物写出来。

The 24 solar terms (二十四节气) divide the solar year — the actual astronomical orbit of the earth around the sun — into 24 equal segments, each roughly 15 days long. Each term has a name describing either a weather phenomenon, an agricultural milestone, or a small natural event: "Awakening of Insects," "Grain Rain," "White Dew," "Frost Descent." The system was codified during the Han dynasty (2nd century BCE) and inscribed in the official almanac. It was used for two thousand years to schedule planting, harvest, and the dishes that follow. UNESCO inscribed it as Intangible Cultural Heritage in 2016. It is, we think, the world's longest continuously running food calendar.二十四节气把太阳年——地球绕太阳的真实轨道——平分成 24 段，每段约 15 天。每个节气有自己的名字，描述天气、农事或一桩小自然事件："惊蛰""谷雨""白露""霜降"。这套系统在汉代（公元前 2 世纪）被编入官方历书，用了两千年——指导播种、收割、以及随之而来的菜。2016 年被联合国教科文组织列入非遗。我们认为它是世界上至今最长寿、还在运行的食物日历。

A complete tour. Each term gets a date, a name, a meaning, and a food. The food is what a North-Chinese household might traditionally eat at this point in the year; the south has its own variations, noted briefly where they differ sharply.完整一圈。每个节气标注日期、名字、含义与一道食物。这道食物是北方家庭传统上在此节气吃的；南方有自己的版本，差异显著时简注。

~Feb 4

立春 Lì chūn · Beginning of spring春之始

Spring pancake (春饼) — a thin wheat crepe rolled around fresh greens, scallion, a dab of fermented bean. Eating greens at lì chūn "bites the spring open."春饼——薄麦皮卷新春菜、葱、一抹甜面酱。立春吃青菜，叫"咬春"。

~Feb 19

雨水 Yǔ shuǐ · Rain water雨水

Bean curd, eaten warm. The first warm-bean season. Sichuan villages braise tofu with the first green peppercorn buds.温食豆腐。雨水后第一波豆腐季。川中乡村以新青花椒嫩芽烧豆腐。

~Mar 5

惊蛰 Jīng zhé · Awakening of insects惊蛰

Pear (梨) — pronounced like "leave" (离). Eaten to "leave" winter's lingering illness behind. Pear-and-rock-sugar soup if you have a cough.吃梨——音近"离"。寓"离开"冬天的病。咳嗽就煮冰糖雪梨。

~Mar 20

春分 Chūn fēn · Spring equinox春分

Spring vegetables — water shield (莼菜), shepherd's purse (荠菜) — at the table. The first wild greens of the year.春菜上桌——莼菜、荠菜。一年里第一波野菜。

~Apr 5

清明 Qīng míng · Clear & bright清明

Qing tuan — see the dedicated essay. Also a moment when the first pre-Qingming green tea (明前茶) is plucked in Hangzhou.青团——详见专篇。也是杭州明前龙井开采的节点。

~Apr 20

谷雨 Gǔ yǔ · Grain rain谷雨

First-flush tea. Sea-coast harvest of mackerel (谷雨春鲅). Sichuan: the first batch of pickled mustard greens of the year.头春茶。海岸第一批春鲅鱼上岸。川地：当年第一坛泡菜下缸。

~May 5

立夏 Lì xià · Beginning of summer立夏

Lì xià rice (立夏饭) — glutinous rice with broad beans, bamboo shoot, and pork. Eaten cold.立夏饭——糯米与蚕豆、笋丁、肉同煮。冷食。

~May 21

小满 Xiǎo mǎn · Grain fills小满

Bitter greens (苦菜) eaten to "clear summer heat" before it arrives. A Cantonese household may begin its first cooling soups of the year.吃苦菜，提前"清热"。粤地人家在此时开始一年的清凉煲汤季。

~Jun 6

芒种 Máng zhòng · Grain in beard芒种

Plum wine (青梅酒) begins; the wheat is harvested. Plums are macerated in shaoxing for the year ahead.青梅酒入坛；麦熟。青梅泡黄酒，备一年。

~Jun 21

夏至 Xià zhì · Summer solstice夏至

Cold noodles (凉面). The North-Chinese saying: "Dongzhi dumplings, Xiazhi noodles." The longest day asks for the lightest meal.凉面。北方俗谚："冬至饺子夏至面。"最长的日子配最轻的饭。

~Jul 7

小暑 Xiǎo shǔ · Slight heat小暑

Mango (south), watermelon, sour plum drink (酸梅汤). The Yunnan mushroom season opens.芒果（南）、西瓜、酸梅汤。云南菌季开锅。

~Jul 22

大暑 Dà shǔ · Great heat大暑

"Eat ginger in summer" — counterintuitively, warming foods reset the body. Cantonese: cooling herbal teas in earnest."夏吃姜"——看似反常的"以热制热"。粤地：凉茶季的正经开始。

~Aug 7

立秋 Lì qiū · Beginning of autumn立秋

"Stick the autumn fat" (贴秋膘) — a heavier meal of red-braised pork or roast duck to ready the body for cooler months.贴秋膘——红烧肉或烤鸭，给身体"上膘"。

~Aug 23

处暑 Chǔ shǔ · End of heat处暑

Duck (鸭) — Nanjing's salted duck is at its best. Yangcheng Lake hairy crabs begin appearing.鸭——南京盐水鸭此时最好。阳澄湖大闸蟹开始上市。

~Sep 7

白露 Bái lù · White dew白露

Longans, pears, the first Suzhou autumn tea. Fujian: salted longan tea for the elderly.龙眼、梨、苏州秋茶上市。福建：老人喝桂圆茶。

~Sep 23

秋分 Qiū fēn · Autumn equinox秋分

Mooncake season runs from here through Mid-Autumn. Suzhou's fresh-pork mooncakes appear in shops.月饼季从这里开始到中秋。苏州鲜肉月饼上柜。

~Oct 8

寒露 Hán lù · Cold dew寒露

Yangcheng Lake hairy crabs at peak. A Jiangnan household eats two crabs a person, with vinegar-ginger.大闸蟹最佳。江南一户两人均两只，配姜醋。

~Oct 23

霜降 Shuāng jiàng · Frost descent霜降

Persimmon (柿). The Xi'an "goose-fat persimmon cake" is at its absolute peak.柿子。西安鹅油柿子饼此时绝佳。

~Nov 7

立冬 Lì dōng · Beginning of winter立冬

Hot pot season opens. Northern households start the suancai jar; the first lap mei is hung.火锅季正式开。北方人家开始下酸菜缸；第一批腊味挂上灶台。

~Nov 22

小雪 Xiǎo xuě · Small snow小雪

Wind-dried meat (风干肉) production begins. Pickled radish jars sealed.风干肉开始做。腌萝卜入缸封口。

~Dec 7

大雪 Dà xuě · Heavy snow大雪

Sausage hanging season. Cantonese lap cheong are ready by month's end.挂腊肠的季节。粤式腊肠月底成熟。

~Dec 21

冬至 Dōng zhì · Winter solstice冬至

See the dedicated essay. Dumplings north, tang yuan south, mutton everywhere.详见专篇。北饺子、南汤圆、全国羊汤。

~Jan 5

小寒 Xiǎo hán · Slight cold小寒

Laba congee (腊八粥) — a thick eight-grain porridge eaten on the 8th of the 12th lunar month, near this term.腊八粥——腊月初八的八种杂粮稠粥，落在此节附近。

~Jan 20

大寒 Dà hán · Great cold大寒

Final winter strengthening. Sticky-rice cake (年糕) production for Spring Festival begins. The year closes.最后的冬补。家家开始为春节做年糕。一年合拢。

The argument that closes the calendar合上日历前的一句话

Why this still matters.为什么这事至今要紧

A modern Chinese city in 2026 does not, of course, plant or harvest. A Shanghai apartment kitchen can buy raspberries from Mexico in February and crab from Norway in June. The 24 solar terms could, by simple economic logic, have died decades ago. They did not. They survive because the calendar is also a piece of language — a way of marking the year that does not collapse into "Q1 / Q2 / Q3 / Q4." When a Beijing grandmother on Bai Lu says she is going to brew longan tea for her grandson, she is not really expressing a botanical opinion. She is locating herself in a year that has 24 distinct moments, each with its own light and its own smell. We argue, gently, for keeping that.2026 年的中国城市当然不种地、不秋收。上海一间公寓厨房二月可以买墨西哥树莓、六月可以买挪威螃蟹。从纯经济逻辑看，二十四节气几十年前就该死了。它没死。它活着，是因为节气同时是一种语言——一种不会把一年崩塌为 Q1/Q2/Q3/Q4 的标年法。白露这天北京一位奶奶说她要给孙子煮桂圆茶，她讲的不是植物学。她在把自己安放在一年里的二十四个不同时刻——每一刻有自己的光与自己的气味。我们温和地主张：留住这套语言。
