Spices & aromatics香料与辛香

The Chinese spice cabinet is smaller than the Indian one but more architectural. Each spice does one job; combinations are written, not improvised. Here is the cabinet, top shelf down.中国香料柜比印度的小，但结构感更强。每一味干一件事，组合是被"写下来"的，不是临时即兴的。下面是从上排往下，一格一格的介绍。

A Chinese spice cabinet is, in theory, just a wooden tray with eight to twelve labelled bags. In practice the same eight bags reproduce themselves across an entire continent of dishes. The defining trick of Chinese aromatics is not the number of spices but the architecture: each spice has a specific role — pungency, perfume, color, depth, an after-warmth — and combinations are codified rather than vibes-based. A typical "five-spice" or "thirteen-spice" blend has a fixed ratio that a serious cook will not vary by more than 10%.理论上，中国的香料柜不过是一个木格盘装着八到十二只小袋。实际上，这同样八只袋子衍生出一整片大陆的菜。中国辛香的关键不在数量，而在结构——每一味负责一件具体的事：辣、香、色、底、回温——配比是被写定的，不是凭感觉的。一个像样的五香或十三香配方比例固定，靠谱的厨子调时不会变动超过 10%。

1 · The Sichuan peppercorn (花椒)一 · 花椒

Not a peppercorn. Not even closely related. Sichuan peppercorn is the dried fruit husk of Zanthoxylum (a citrus-family bush) and it produces an electric, vibratory tingle on the lips and tongue rather than heat — the sensation is called ma (麻, "numbness"). The active compound is hydroxy-alpha-sanshool. Two main varieties: red Sichuan peppercorn (花椒) is more aromatic and the standard; green Sichuan peppercorn (青花椒) is sharper, citrusy, with a longer tingle. Best from Hanyuan, in southwestern Sichuan, harvested late July to mid-August. Use whole, briefly toasted in oil, then either left in the dish or strained out. A pinch of freshly-ground powder, applied at the end, brings back the volatile aromatics that cooking has driven off.不是胡椒。两者也不近亲。花椒是芸香科植物的干果壳，给的不是热感，而是嘴唇与舌头上的"电流"——这种感觉叫"麻"。活性物质是羟基-α-山椒素。主要两种：红花椒香气更厚，是标准用法；青花椒更锐利、带柠檬味，麻的持续时间更长。最好的产自川西南汉源，七月底到八月中采。整粒用，下油锅快煸；之后或留或滤。一撮新磨花椒粉，出锅前撒，能补回煮的过程中跑掉的挥发香气。

2 · Star anise (八角)二 · 八角

Eight-pointed star pods from a southern Chinese evergreen, Illicium verum. The dominant aromatic is anethole — the same molecule that scents fennel, anise, and absinthe. The smell is what most Western cooks read as the "Chinese" smell, because the spice anchors red-braised pork (红烧肉), master stocks (老汤), and almost any long simmer. Use one or two pods per pot; more is not better. The Chinese kitchen treats star anise as warm bass, not foreground.中国南方常绿灌木 Illicium verum 的八角形干果。主导香气物质是茴香脑——与小茴香、欧茴香、苦艾酒同一种分子。这是大部分西方厨房读为"中国味"的那种气味，因为它撑起了红烧肉、老汤、几乎所有的长炖。一锅放一到两颗，多了不见得好。中餐把八角当成温厚的"低音"，不是主角。

3 · Cassia bark (桂皮)三 · 桂皮

A thicker, woodier, more aggressive cousin of true cinnamon. Cassia is what Chinese kitchens mean by "cinnamon," and it goes into the same long-simmer dishes as star anise — five-spice powder, lou shui brine, red-braised pork. Best in 2–3 cm chunks rather than ground; the active cinnamaldehyde releases slowly during cooking.桂皮——比真正的肉桂更厚、更木、更冲的近亲。中餐讲"肉桂"通常就是它。它和八角一起进同一类长炖菜——五香粉、卤水、红烧肉。最好整段 2–3 厘米用，别磨粉；肉桂醛在长炖中慢慢释放。

4 · Fennel seed (小茴香)四 · 小茴香

Foeniculum vulgare, the same plant Italian sausage uses. In Chinese cooking, it works alongside cumin for lamb (especially in the Northwest) and is one of the five base spices in five-spice powder. Toasted briefly in a dry pan before grinding, the aroma changes character — from grassy to honeyed.小茴香 (Foeniculum vulgare)，与意大利香肠里用的是同一种植物。在中餐里，它与孜然一起处理羊肉（尤其西北），也是五香粉里的五味之一。下锅前先干锅短烤再研磨，香气从青草味变成蜜味。

5 · Cumin (孜然)五 · 孜然

Not historically Chinese — cumin arrived from Central Asia via the Silk Road and stayed in the Northwest. Today it is the defining seasoning of Uyghur lamb, Xi'an grilled lamb, and a small but growing band of Sichuan dishes that borrow its smoky-warm character. Use whole or coarsely cracked; powdered cumin loses its perfume within weeks.不是历史上原生的中国香料——孜然经丝路从中亚传入，落脚在西北。今天它是维吾尔羊肉、西安烤肉的定调；近年也有一批川菜借它那种烟熏温暖的性格。整粒用或粗碎；磨成粉后香气几周就消散。

6 · Cloves (丁香)六 · 丁香

A dangerous spice. One clove perfumes a pot. Two cloves dominate it. The active compound eugenol is so volatile that whole cloves are used and then removed; ground clove almost never appears in serious Chinese cooking. The one place to splurge: a master stock for goose or pork, where a single clove can sit for the entire 90-minute braise and disappear behind the other spices, leaving only its trail.一种危险的香料。一颗能调香一锅，两颗就抢戏。活性物丁子香酚太挥发，所以中餐基本都是整颗放进去、再取出来；磨粉的丁香几乎不出现在认真的中菜里。可以放手用的地方：卤鹅或卤肉的老汤里——一颗丁香在 90 分钟的卤里慢慢释放，只留下一道气味的痕迹。

7 · Dried tangerine peel (陈皮)七 · 陈皮

Possibly the most underappreciated spice on the Chinese shelf. Dried, aged tangerine peel — typically from the Xinhui mandarin of Guangdong — is sold by the year of aging: three-year peel is everyday, ten-year is special, twenty-five-year is gifted in lacquered boxes. The aging concentrates volatile oils and softens the bitter edge. Used in soups, master stocks, and a particular preparation of red-bean soup that improves a million-fold if you remember to add a 2 cm strip.中国香料柜里最被低估的一味。陈皮——多取广东新会柑——按年份卖：三年的日常用，十年的是讲究，二十五年的装漆盒送人。陈化让挥发油浓缩、苦味变柔。用于煲汤、老卤、以及一种红豆汤——如果你记得放一段 2 厘米陈皮，那碗红豆汤好上一千倍。

8 · Bay leaf (香叶) & sand ginger (沙姜)八 · 香叶与沙姜

Bay leaf (Cinnamomum tamala — Chinese bay, related to true bay but with a slight cinnamon edge) shows up in five-spice and braises. Sand ginger (Kaempferia galanga) is a small dried rhizome with a piney, almost camphor-like aroma; it is the secret ingredient in salt-baked Hakka chicken and a few Cantonese roast preparations.香叶（Cinnamomum tamala，中国香叶，与真正的月桂近亲但带桂皮香）出现在五香粉与卤里。沙姜（Kaempferia galanga）是一种小型干根茎，香气里有松脂与一点樟脑——客家盐焗鸡与几样粤式烧腊里的"秘密武器"。

9 · Aromatics: the holy trinity九 · 辛香三件套

If the dried-spice cabinet is the bass section, the fresh aromatics are the lead. The Chinese "holy trinity" of garlic, ginger, and scallion appears at the start of probably 70% of Chinese savory dishes. Each plays a distinct role: garlic is the round sweetness when fried in oil; ginger cuts grease and lifts the high notes; scallion provides green-onion sharpness and color. A wok cook will tell you the order matters — usually ginger first (the most heat-tolerant), then garlic, then scallion last so it doesn't burn. The Cantonese variant adds a fourth: shallot. The Sichuan variant adds dried chili.如果说干香料柜是低音，鲜辛香就是主旋律。中餐"葱姜蒜"三件套大约出现在 70% 的咸菜起锅里。各司其职：蒜下热油后给出圆润的甜；姜削腻、提高音；葱给青味与色。锅工都会告诉你顺序——姜先（耐高温），蒜中，葱末了（不易煳）。粤菜加第四样：干葱头（红葱头）。川菜加干辣椒。

The classical blends两个经典配方

Five-spice powder (五香粉) — the canonical blend: star anise, cassia, fennel, clove, Sichuan peppercorn, ground at a ratio of roughly 4:2:2:1:2. Used in roast meats and master stocks. Thirteen-spice (十三香) — the more elaborate cousin: the above plus white pepper, sand ginger, dried ginger, bay leaf, nutmeg, cardamom, and dried tangerine peel. Used in dumpling fillings, slow stews, and grandmother's secret meatball recipes. Both blends should be made fresh; pre-mixed bags lose their volatile compounds within months.五香粉——经典配方：八角、桂皮、小茴、丁香、花椒，比例约 4:2:2:1:2。用于烧腊与老卤。十三香——更繁复的版本：以上加白胡椒、沙姜、干姜、香叶、肉豆蔻、白豆蔻、陈皮。用于饺子馅、慢炖、奶奶秘传肉丸子。两种配方都该新磨；袋装预混的几个月就跑香。
